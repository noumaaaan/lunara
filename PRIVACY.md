# Privacy Policy for Lunara

**Effective date:** 16 April 2026

Lunara is a private, ad-free dream journal.

## Information Collection
Lunara does not require an account and does not collect your dream entries on external servers.

## Data Storage
Dream entries and related information are stored locally on your device.

## Advertising
Lunara does not display third-party advertising.

## Analytics
Lunara does not use analytics to track your personal activity.

## External Links
If you use feedback or support links, Lunara may open external apps or websites such as your mail app. Those services operate under their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy
Lunara is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
This privacy policy may be updated in future versions of Lunara. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
