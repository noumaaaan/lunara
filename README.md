# Lunara Dreams

Lunara is a calm, beautifully simple dream journal designed to help you capture dreams before they fade — completely private, ad-free, and free to use.

Log dreams quickly when you wake up, revisit them later in your journal, explore them by date in your calendar, and notice gentle patterns over time through insights. Lunara is designed to feel quiet, personal, and easy to return to, whether you want to keep a casual dream diary or build a deeper habit of reflection.

Your dreams stay yours. Lunara does not require an account, does not show ads, and is designed to keep your entries personal and private on your device.


## Features
- Log dreams quickly with a simple two-step flow
- Add details like date, category, intensity, and waking mood
- Revisit past dreams in your journal
- Browse entries by date with a dedicated calendar view
- See gentle patterns through dream insights and activity trends
- No ads, no account, and no unnecessary distractions
- Free to use
- Designed to keep your entries personal and private on your device

## Privacy

Lunara is built for thoughtful dream logging — simple enough to use every morning, calm enough to grow with over time. Your entries are kept personal and private.

- No account required
- No ads
- Dream entries are stored locally on your device
- No cloud sync in the current version

You can read the privacy policy here: [Privacy Policy](https://github.com/noumaaaan/lunara/blob/main/PRIVACY.md)
