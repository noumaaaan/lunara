# Lunara

Lunara is a calm, beautifully simple dream journal designed to help you capture dreams before they fade.

Log dreams quickly when you wake up, revisit them later in your journal, explore them by date in your calendar, and notice gentle patterns over time through insights.

## Features

- Quick two-step dream logging
- Add details like date, category, intensity, and waking mood
- Revisit past dreams in your journal
- Browse entries by date in a dedicated calendar view
- Notice gentle patterns through insights and activity trends
- Private, ad-free, and free to use

## Privacy

Lunara is designed to keep your entries personal and private.

- No account required
- No ads
- Dream entries are stored locally on your device
- No cloud sync in the current version
